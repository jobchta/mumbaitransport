WhatsApp Ticketing

Line 1 - Blue Line

Follow 4 simple steps to buy a WhatsApp e-ticket:

  1. Send 'hi' to 9670008889
  2. Click on the link to select: source & destination.
  3. Click to pay online for e-ticket (Or Share OTP to purchase Paper Ticket at the ticketing counters, with cash/ QR).
  4. Payment ones successful, Receive WhatsApp link with QR to travel!

Lines 2A & 7 - Yellow & Red Lines

To book a WhatsApp ticket:

  1. Scan the QR code or send "Hi" on 86526 35500
  2. An automatic reply will be provided through Chatbot with an option to "Buy Ticket" / Retrieve Ticket / Last Transaction
  3. Select the option to "Buy Ticket"
  4. An external link will be provided to select the origin and Destination and make payment using Bank Card or UPI
